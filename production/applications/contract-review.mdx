## Overview

A contract review agent that analyzes legal documents, extracts key terms and obligations, and flags potential risks or unusual clauses.

## Planned Features

- Key term extraction (dates, amounts, parties)
- Obligation and deadline identification
- Risk flagging (non-standard clauses, liability concerns)
- Comparison against standard templates
- Summary generation for quick review
- Redlining suggestions

## Use Cases

- Vendor contract review
- NDA analysis
- Employment agreement review
- Lease and rental agreements
- Service level agreement validation
